BCM Toolkit Ui refresh

Overview

To make the transition from Drupal7 to Drupal10 easier for their users, Morgan Solus hired me to redesign their BCM toolkit site. I refreshed the colours to match their branding and used CSS and HTML to implement the changes. I also refreshed the login pages with CSS and HTML and helped with layout changes. Working on this project throughout the year has helped me solidify my confidence in implementing my designs and I am so grateful for that experience.This project has also given me insight into the world of Drupal and how to modify it.
